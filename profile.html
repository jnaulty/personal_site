Future Goals
After graduation I plan on continuing my pursuit of studying the mind via Brain-Machine Interfaces. I aim to integrate computers with the mind to help make mindful practices more accessible, and trainable, to everyday people. I believe that with the help of biofeedback devices, people will be able to gain a better sense of their bodies' condition. Increasing awareness of one's own body can help with decisions regarding one's health. Integrating machines into the process allows for data collection, which can be interpreted by oneself, along with one's physician, to get a better understanding of one's health in real-time. I am currently looking for institutions involved in this form of research.
